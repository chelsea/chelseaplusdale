Getting here

International

There are three main options for flights, you can fly to Sydney or Melbourne and rent a car (or take a very long busy trip) to drive around 7 hours to our place. Alternatively, you can fly to Canberra from Sydney or Melbourne and have a 3 hour drive to our place. A more expensive but time saving option is to fly into Merimbula with Rex, however this is small regional airline with tight baggage limits, limited schedule, and expensive flights.

From Australia

For flights (Perth is pretty much international!), see the details for international visitors.
Driving from Sydney you can take the scenic, shorter, but more variable (more traffic hold ups, speed limits drop to 50km/h) coast road, or head inland along the Hume via Canberra. The Hume will be a bit longer, but a more straightforward drive. We'd recommend taking the scenic coast road if you haven't before.
We'll be making trips in and out of Bega and Merimbula/Pambula leading up to the wedding and should be able to co-ordinate pick ups from buses or the airpor there as needed.

Accommodation

We'll be doing our best to arrange a bus, shuttle, or some other form of transport for guests on the day of the wedding from/to Merimbula, as the accommodation options there are much better than in Pambula as are the general attractions for out of towners.
We'll also have an area for camping on the property, however we ask that aside from the wedding day you come prepared to be self sufficient with food, water, etc.

On the day

Our wedding is going to be on the informal side, and outdoors. We'll have a marquee tent for some shade and general shelter, the weather in November is typically quite nice during the day with possibly a cooler evening.
Events will kick off around lunch time, with the ceremony scheduled for some time that day.
We'll be roasting a pig on a spit (maybe also a lamb) for the meals, please let us know in advance if this is an issue and we can sort out alternative mains as needed.
